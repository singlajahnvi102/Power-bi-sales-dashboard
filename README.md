# Northwind Sales Performance Dashboard

This project analyzes sales performance for Northwind Traders using Power BI.

The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, product performance, customer contributions, and shipping costs.

## Business Questions Solved

1. Are there noticeable sales trends over time?
   - The dashboard includes a Monthly Revenue Trend chart showing revenue patterns across months.

2. Which products generate the most revenue?
   - A Top Products by Revenue chart identifies the highest performing products.

3. Who are the most valuable customers?
   - A Top Customers by Revenue treemap highlights the customers contributing the most to total revenue.

4. Are shipping costs consistent across providers?
   - A comparison of Average Shipping Cost by Shipping Provider shows cost differences between shipping companies.

5. How much revenue comes from discontinued products?
   - A chart analyzing revenue generated by discontinued products provides additional business insights.

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: $1.27M
- Total Orders: 830
- Total Customers: 91
- Average Shipping Cost: 78.24
